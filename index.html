About Us

At Aarogyasarva healthcare , we are focused on providing high quality primary care in low cost to the poor and underprivileged communities in maharashtra.

We provide our patients with medical consultation at Rs.80 and 1-week supply of medications for free. We also provide subsidized spectacles. Cataract and pterygium surgeries are also provided for free, in collaboration with Rotary Eye Hospitals, with patients only bearing the travelling expenses.

vision and mission

our Vision
We seek a world of hope that is inclusive and just, where all people live in dignity and security.

our mission
Our mission is to eradicate the lack of health care facilities in rural India by creating a chain of primary health care units that offer high quality, affordable outpatient care in low income, densely populated regions, where health care delivery by the state is sparse.
